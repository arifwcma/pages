Privacy Policy

Social Monitor — operated by Wimmera Catchment Management Authority (WCMA), Horsham, Victoria, Australia.

Last updated: 19 March 2026

What This App Does

Social Monitor monitors comments on the WCMA Facebook page for offensive or inappropriate content. It scores each comment using AI and sends an internal email alert to WCMA staff when a comment exceeds a configured severity threshold.

Data We Access

The app accesses publicly visible comments and commenter display names on the WCMA Facebook page. We do not access private messages, personal profiles, friend lists, or any data beyond page-level comment content.

How Data Is Used

Comment text is sent to OpenAI's API for sentiment analysis. The resulting score and comment details are included in internal email alerts sent exclusively to authorised WCMA staff. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data Storage

Comment data is processed in real time and is not permanently stored. Temporary log files may be retained for debugging purposes and are deleted regularly.

Data Retention

We do not maintain a database of comments or user information. Email alerts are retained in the recipient's mailbox according to WCMA's standard email retention policies.

User Rights

If you have questions about how your comment data is handled, or wish to request removal, contact us at the address below.
